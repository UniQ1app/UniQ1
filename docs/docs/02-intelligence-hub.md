# Intelligence Hub — Public Interface

The Intelligence Hub is the public entry point of UNIQ.
No login is required.

Its purpose is to:
- Kill misinformation (FUD)
- Educate newcomers
- Provide real-time, verified signals about Qubic

This interface must be useful even for users who do not own tokens.

---

## Core Modules

### 1. Market Pulse

A contextual market overview, not just a price chart.

Displayed data:
- QUBIC price
- Market capitalization
- Trading volume
- Burn indicator (deflation visualization)

AI Intelligence:
- Detects correlation with global crypto market
- Explains price movement in simple language
  (e.g. “Market-wide correction” vs “Qubic-specific event”)
- Social sentiment score (Fear / Neutral / Optimism)

---

### 2. Ecosystem Map

A live map of everything built on Qubic.

Displayed data:
- List of projects
- Category (Wallet, DEX, AI, Gaming, Infra)
- Status: Concept / In Development / Live

AI Intelligence:
- Reads technical updates (GitHub, changelogs)
- Translates them into human-readable summaries

---

### 3. Opportunity Board

A passive talent marketplace.

Displayed data:
- Open needs from projects
- Available bounties (off-chain in Phase 1)

AI Intelligence:
- Highlights critical bottlenecks in the ecosystem
- Surfaces high-impact opportunities automatically

---

### 4. Deep Insights

Long-form knowledge and analysis.

Displayed data:
- Educational articles
- Technical deep dives
- Competitive analysis

AI Intelligence:
- Auto-generated summaries (TL;DR)
- Key takeaways for non-technical users

---

### 5. Official Communications

Single source of truth.

Displayed data:
- Core team announcements
- Network upgrades
- Protocol updates

AI Intelligence:
- Fact-checking engine
- Answers trending questions using verified sources only

---

## Design Principles

- Signal over noise
- No speculation incentives
- No comment wars
- Truth must be visible and structured
