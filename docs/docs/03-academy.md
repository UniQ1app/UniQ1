## Learning Structure

The Academy is divided into structured learning paths:

- Qubic Fundamentals
- Distributed Computing & AI
- Economic Model & Incentives
- Governance Philosophy

Each path is composed of:
- Lessons
- Quizzes
- Progress tracking

---

## AI Learning Assistant

An AI tutor assists users by:
- Explaining complex concepts in simple language
- Generating practice questions
- Detecting weak knowledge areas before exams

The goal is not memorization, but understanding.

---

## Master Gate (Qualification Exam)

The Master Gate is the transition point between learning and responsibility.

To access it:
- The user must complete required courses
- A qualification fee may be required (off-chain in Phase 1)

Exam properties:
- Dynamically generated questions
- Difficulty adapts to network demand
- Anti-cheat mechanisms (question randomization, time limits)

Passing the Master Gate unlocks higher roles in Phase 2.

---

## Phase 1 Limitation

In Phase 1:
- Exams are simulated
- Results are stored off-chain
- No blockchain interaction occurs
